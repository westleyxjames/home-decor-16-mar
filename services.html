Our Services

From intimate consultations to comprehensive home transformations, we offer a range of services designed to meet you wherever you are in your design journey.

Virtual Styling

$750

Perfect for those seeking expert guidance without a full redesign. Our virtual styling service connects you with our design team from anywhere in the world.

During our comprehensive 90-minute session, we'll discuss your space, aesthetic preferences, and functional needs. You'll receive a custom mood board, detailed product recommendations, and a clear roadmap for implementation.

What's Included:

  • 90-minute video consultation
  • Personalized mood board
  • Curated product recommendations
  • Color palette guidance
  • Implementation roadmap

Room Curation

$2,500

Transform a single space with our signature attention to detail. This service includes an in-person consultation, professional space planning, and curated furniture and décor selection.

We handle everything from initial measurements and 3D renderings to furniture procurement and final styling. The result is a cohesive, beautifully designed room that exceeds your expectations.

What's Included:

  • In-person consultation & measurements
  • 3D renderings & space planning
  • Custom furniture sourcing
  • Décor & accessory curation
  • Installation & final styling

Full Home Redesign

$7,500

Our most comprehensive offering, perfect for those embarking on a complete home transformation. This service encompasses every aspect of interior design, from concept development to final installation.

We manage the entire process, coordinating with contractors, sourcing premium furnishings and art, and overseeing installation. The result is a cohesive, magazine-worthy home that reflects your unique style and enhances your daily life.

What's Included:

  • Comprehensive design development
  • Premium furniture & art curation
  • Full project management
  • Vendor & contractor coordination
  • White-glove installation & styling

How We Work

Our Process

A thoughtful, collaborative approach that ensures your vision is realized with precision and care at every step.

1. Discovery

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your lifestyle, aesthetic preferences, and functional requirements.

2. Concept Development

Our team develops a comprehensive design concept, including mood boards, color palettes, and initial product selections.

3. Design Refinement

Through collaborative reviews, we refine the design, ensuring every element aligns with your vision.

4. Procurement

We handle all purchasing, coordination, and logistics, working with our trusted network of vendors and artisans.

5. Installation

Our team oversees delivery and installation, ensuring every piece is placed with care and precision.

6. Final Styling

We add the finishing touches—art, accessories, and styling details—that bring the entire design together.
